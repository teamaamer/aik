About AK Auto

A family-owned automotive spare parts business built on trust, experience, and long-term customer relationships.

Our Story

Founded on Family.
Built on Trust.

AK Auto was founded in 1990 by Ahmad Ibrahim Khalil Amer and has grown as a family-owned automotive spare parts business focused on quality, trust, and long-term customer relationships. The name AK carries the founder's initials — a personal mark that reflects the responsibility behind every part we supply.

With decades of market experience, AK Auto supports drivers, workshops, and businesses in finding the right parts with confidence. We are not a generic catalogue — we are a team that knows parts, knows customers, and stands behind every part we supply.

35+ Years in Business Family-Owned Global Suppliers

Our mission: To provide reliable auto parts and professional support that helps customers choose the right part with confidence.
